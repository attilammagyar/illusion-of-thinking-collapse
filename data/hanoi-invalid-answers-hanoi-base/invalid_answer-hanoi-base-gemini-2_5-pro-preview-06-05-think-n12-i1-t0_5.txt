### The Strategy

To move a stack of $n$ disks from a source peg to a destination peg, we can follow these three steps:
1.  Move the top $n-1$ disks from the source peg to the auxiliary peg.
2.  Move the single largest disk ($n$) from the source peg to the destination peg.
3.  Move the $n-1$ disks from the auxiliary peg to the destination peg.

This process is repeated until we are only moving a single disk. For a puzzle with $n$ disks, this will take $2^n - 1$ moves. In your case, with 12 disks, the total number of moves will be $2^{12} - 1 = 4095$.

### Strategy
The classic solution for the Tower of Hanoi involves recursion:
- To move `n` disks from Peg A to Peg C using Peg B as auxiliary:
  1. Move `n-1` disks from Peg A to Peg B using Peg C as auxiliary.
  2. Move the nth disk (the largest one) from Peg A to Peg C.
  3. Move the `n-1` disks from Peg B to Peg C using Peg A as auxiliary.

This approach ensures that we never place a larger disk on a smaller one.

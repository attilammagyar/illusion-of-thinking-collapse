Let's solve the classic **Tower of Hanoi** puzzle with 8 disks, using the recursive algorithm.

### **Approach**

For \( n \) disks, move from peg A (0) to peg C (2) using peg B (1) as auxiliary:

1. Move \( n-1 \) disks from A to B (using C as auxiliary).
2. Move disk \( n \) from A to C.
3. Move \( n-1 \) disks from B to C (using A as auxiliary).

We'll record each move as `[disk id, from peg, to peg]`, where the disk id is 1 (smallest) to 8 (largest).

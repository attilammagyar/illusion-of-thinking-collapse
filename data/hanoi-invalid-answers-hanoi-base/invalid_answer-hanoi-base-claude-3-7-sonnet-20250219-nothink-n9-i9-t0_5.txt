# Tower of Hanoi Solution for 9 Disks

This is the classic Tower of Hanoi puzzle with 9 disks. I'll solve this step by step.

## Solution Approach

The Tower of Hanoi has a recursive solution. For n disks:
1. Move n-1 disks from source to auxiliary peg
2. Move the largest disk from source to destination
3. Move n-1 disks from auxiliary to destination

For 9 disks, we need 2^9 - 1 = 511 moves (minimum required).

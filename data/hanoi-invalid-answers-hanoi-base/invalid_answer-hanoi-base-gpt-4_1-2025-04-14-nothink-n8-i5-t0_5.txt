### Approach

The Tower of Hanoi solution is recursive:

- Move \( n-1 \) disks from source to auxiliary peg.
- Move the largest disk to the destination peg.
- Move the \( n-1 \) disks from auxiliary to destination peg.

I'll provide the **complete list of moves** in the format:  
`[disk id, from peg, to peg]`  
where disk 1 is the smallest, disk 8 is the largest.

## Solution

Let's define a function for clarity:

```python
def hanoi(n, source, target, auxiliary, moves):
    if n == 0:
        return
    hanoi(n-1, source, auxiliary, target, moves)
    moves.append([n, source, target])
    hanoi(n-1, auxiliary, target, source, moves)
```

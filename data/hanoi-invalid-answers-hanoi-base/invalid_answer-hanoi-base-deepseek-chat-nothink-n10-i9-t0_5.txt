### Recursive Approach

The Tower of Hanoi problem is classically solved using recursion. The recursive solution can be summarized as:

1. Move the top \( n-1 \) disks from the source peg to the auxiliary peg (not the target peg).
2. Move the \( n^{th} \) disk (the largest one) from the source peg to the target peg.
3. Move the \( n-1 \) disks from the auxiliary peg to the target peg.

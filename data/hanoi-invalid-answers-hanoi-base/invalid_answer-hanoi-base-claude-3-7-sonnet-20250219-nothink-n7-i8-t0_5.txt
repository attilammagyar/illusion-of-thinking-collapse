This is the classic Tower of Hanoi puzzle with 7 disks. I'll solve it step by step using the recursive approach.

## Recursive Solution Approach

The Tower of Hanoi has an elegant recursive solution:
1. To move n disks from source to destination using an auxiliary peg:
   - Move n-1 disks from source to auxiliary
   - Move the largest disk from source to destination
   - Move n-1 disks from auxiliary to destination

# Tower of Hanoi Solution for 10 Disks

This is the classic Tower of Hanoi puzzle with 10 disks. I'll solve it systematically.

## Approach

The Tower of Hanoi has a recursive solution:
1. To move n disks from source to destination using an auxiliary peg:
   - Move n-1 disks from source to auxiliary
   - Move the largest disk from source to destination
   - Move n-1 disks from auxiliary to destination

For 10 disks, we need to:
- Move disks 1-9 from peg 0 to peg 1 (using peg 2 as auxiliary)
- Move disk 10 from peg 0 to peg 2
- Move disks 1-9 from peg 1 to peg 2 (using peg 0 as auxiliary)

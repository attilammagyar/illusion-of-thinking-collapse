### The Strategy

To move a stack of $n$ disks from a source peg to a destination peg, we follow these three steps:
1.  Move the top $n-1$ disks from the **source** peg to the **auxiliary** peg.
2.  Move the single largest disk ($n$) from the **source** peg to the **destination** peg.
3.  Move the $n-1$ disks from the **auxiliary** peg to the **destination** peg.

This process is repeated until we are only moving a single disk. For 10 disks, this results in $2^{10} - 1 = 1023$ moves.

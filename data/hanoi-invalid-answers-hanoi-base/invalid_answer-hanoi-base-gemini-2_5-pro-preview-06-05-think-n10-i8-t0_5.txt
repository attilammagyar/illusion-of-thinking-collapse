1.  Move $n-1$ disks from the source peg to the auxiliary peg.
2.  Move the $n$-th disk (the largest one) from the source peg to the destination peg.
3.  Move the $n-1$ disks from the auxiliary peg to the destination peg.

2.  Move disk 10 from peg 0 to peg 2.
3.  Move 9 disks from peg 1 to peg 2.
